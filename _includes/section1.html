I'm Helen, and I'm currently working as a postdoctoral scholar at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography, where I am the Data Manager for the International Ocean Discovery Program Science Support Office. We manage proposals and site survey data throughout a rigorous review process in order to implement scientific ocean drilling expeditions.

My background is in marine geophysics. I got my PhD from the MIT/WHOI Joint Program in Oceanography, using active-source seismic data to study how the seafloor formed and evolved under various geologic conditions. I enjoy all aspects of working with data, from collecting and organizing diverse datasets to analyzing and gaining meaningful insights relevant to the world.
